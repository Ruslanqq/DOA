About Us
DOA Trading DMCC. Gathered experience, high qualified staff affords us to take leading positions among companies on the territory of Central Asia. For more full satisfaction of needs of partners, we are growing producing capacity step-by- step. Our branches offer to their consumer a great range of agricultural production both in inside the market, and on markets of CIS and far abroad. We thank our partners, with whom we are growing and perfecting. And those people daily, tensed toil became the reason of our achievements.
Our mission
The mission of DOA Agro company is: Import and export of high qualified, ecological agricultural production and promotion of it both on inner market, and international market. Broadening of the sphere of activity, its intensive moving on international level. Progress and saving of leading positions. Increasing of sales volume in consequence of broadening and increasing the range of agricultural production. Maximal satisfaction of consumer demand and task-oriented service of consumers. Progress of global broadening of the activity of the company.
Our Principles
In our work we follow three main principles: Competence means, that we undertake only that tasks, which we are capable to solve with the help of our knowledge, technologies and experience. Practicability means, that we will never take the project, if we know, that there will be no practical use.We are honest with our partners and are always ready to carry on constructive argues, for finding out a decision of the task. Effectiveness means, which spent expenses, should be retuned both for the company and for their partners.
